About Prime Finish Cleaning Co

Merciful Maids began serving the Triad area in 2019 with a simple mission: provide reliable, high-quality cleaning services that help homes, businesses, and properties stay clean, organized, and ready for what comes next.

From 2019 through early 2026 the company operated under the name Merciful Maids. During that time we built experience cleaning homes, rental properties, and commercial spaces across the Triad while developing the systems and standards that guide our work today.

In 2026 the business evolved into Prime Finish Cleaning Co to reflect a broader vision: supporting homeowners, property managers, contractors, and businesses with dependable cleaning services ranging from routine home maintenance to post-construction cleanup.

What started as a small local cleaning operation has grown into a service focused on consistency, professionalism, and attention to detail on every job. Every space we clean is treated with care so clients can enjoy a fresh environment to live, work, and build in.
